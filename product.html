The product:

Quiz to recommend new products to user

  • Upon registering for an account, the user is asked a series of questions (a very short quiz - max 10 questions) that will then decipher what sort of products they may want.

  • Thsese products are decided by AI - based on much data inputs. Each product will have the option for a smiley face, a sad face or a blank face, each grading the product recommendation as good, bad or mediocre (respectively).

  • Machine learning could then be used to recommend new products and change any recommendations made for other users.

  • Potential questions:

      1: Do you like problem solving or more straightforward tasks?
      2: Do you like working outdoors?
      3: Do you always forget to buy things repeatedly?
      4: Are you trying to find more storage or declutter your space?
      5: Are you looking for a more practical phone case? (if yes, please state what phone you have)
      6: Do you want help with chores?
      7: Do you like operating with technological devices or offline devices (i.e. books)?

  • This puts the customer at the centre - a value that is crucial to Amazon - and ensures their happiness with the company by improving convenience and even encouraging them to buy more, thus potentially improving Amazon's revenue.
